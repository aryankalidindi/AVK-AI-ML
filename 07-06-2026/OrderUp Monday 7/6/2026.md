Semi-manual mode is built and ready. Here's how the flow works now:

1. You speak/POST an order → Gemini parses it → the tool auto-opens the right store in the browser (this part is solid).
2. Your phone buzzes: "Store is open — build your cart."
3. You tap your items in the Chrome window (sidestepping the fuzzy-search/virtualized-menu wall that automation can't reliably clear).
4. You hit the new cart-ready endpoint → the tool tries to read the cart total, notifies you to review, and waits.
5. You confirm → dry-run marks it placed (no charge); later, live mode places it.

The full automation framework is untouched behind a CART_MODE=auto switch — when the item/cart selectors get solved, you flip one env var and it's fully hands-free. Nothing was thrown away.
